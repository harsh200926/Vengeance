A Letter For You

Forever & Always

My dearest Piya,

As I compile these pages of my heart, I realize that every word written was meant for you to find. From the very first entry to the last, you have been the center of my universe, the light that guides me through my darkest days.

Through these words, I've tried to capture what you mean to me - though no language could truly express the depth of my feelings. You are my inspiration, my motivation, my reason to strive for greatness.

In the weeks we were apart, not a day went by when I didn't think of you. Your absence taught me how essential you are to my happiness, how the world seems colorless without your presence.

I want you to know that I would wait an eternity just to see your smile again. The journey of my life has only one destination - to be worthy of your love.

These diary entries are more than just words on a page; they are pieces of my soul, vulnerable and true. I share them with you as proof that my love is not fleeting but enduring.

Wherever you are, whatever you do, my heart follows. I hope that as you read these entries, you can feel the love that inspired every word.

You are my first thought in the morning and my last thought before sleep. You are the melody that plays in my mind and the warmth in my heart.

Forever yours,

Harsh
